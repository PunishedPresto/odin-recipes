Chicken Soup Recipe

When the cold season hits make this easy slow cooker chicken noodle soup recipe by adding lean white meat, vegetables, stock, and pasta to your crock-pot.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Chicken Broth
  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 1tbs of Salt+Pepper
  • Egg Noodles
    1. Chop carrots and celery
    2. Mix carrots, celery, chicken, and cover with chicken broth.
    3. Stir in Salt and Pepper
    4. Cook on low for at least 8 hours, or high for 4
    5. Stir in egg noodles until Al Dente, then serve!
